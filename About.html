History

Uniti is a class project for DES680 Digital Design instructed by Richard Hall. The project went through several stages - needfinding, research, concept development, storyboarding, identity development,
user testing - and eventually culminated in the creation
of this website.

Mission

Uniti is devoted to raising awareness for homelessness among children in NYC. Uniti makes it easy for us to learn about homeless children and motivates us to care more. One key aspect of Uniti is create a two-way connection between homeless children and the rest of us.

Inspiration

Uniti has been inspired by a combination of stories and projects and is constantly collecting inspirations from various sources. Among the inspirations are the story Homeless to Harvard: The Liz Murray Story, project Underheard in New York and the story of Ted Williams .

Designer

Uniti is designed and developed by M.J Ma. M.J graduated from Stanford University with a B.S. in Symbolic Systems and is currently a first-year graduate student in M.S. Commu-nications Design at Pratt Institute.
