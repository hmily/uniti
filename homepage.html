Identity

UNITI's identity symbol has three layers of meaning: 1) the globules represent two entities - homeless children and the rest of us; 2) the shapes of the globules encompass each other, meaning that we are unified as a whole; 3) the morphing of both globules signifies that the interaction between us is a two-way interaction.

How does Uniti work?

This video shows where the idea of Uniti came from, and user testing on the prototype. The testing was conducted at a bus stop in Union Square, Manhattan, New York. A total of 14 people interacted with the prototype, with 6 interviewed.
